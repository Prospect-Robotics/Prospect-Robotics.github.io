Support Us

  • Grants

    The main way that we a funded is through grants. We write to lots of major companies such as Walmart, BAE Systems, Western Digital and many more.

  • Demos

    One important way for us to get people interested in robotics is through demonstraions. We will bring out our robot and show off what it can do and how it was made. We do this so that more members of the community are actively searching for a way to help us grow and develop as a team.

  • Donations

    One of the easiest ways to help support us is by making a donation to our GoFundMe campaign. The link can be found below.

  • Mentorship

    Another way to help is through mentorship. We are always looking for parents, teachers, or a volounteers to help teach us. Our mentors are a very important part of designing and fabricating our robots.

  • Machine Shops

    Our team also uses machine shops to make our parts, and we are always open to any new shops wanting to sponsor us.
